Ramen

Description

Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup known for its rich, savory broth and endless customization options. This dish typically features wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, topped with proteins like sliced pork (chashu), soft-boiled eggs, and an assortment of vegetables and seasonings. Ingredients for a basic ramen include ramen noodles, chicken or pork stock, soy sauce, miso paste, garlic, ginger, green onions, mushrooms, nori (seaweed), a soft-boiled egg, and sliced pork or tofu for vegetarians.

To prepare ramen, start by making the broth. Combine chicken or pork stock with soy sauce, miso paste, grated garlic, and ginger in a pot, and let it simmer for 30 minutes to develop a rich flavor. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions and drain them. In a serving bowl, arrange the noodles and pour the hot broth over them. Top with sliced pork or tofu, soft-boiled eggs, sliced mushrooms, chopped green onions, and a piece of nori. Serve hot, and feel free to add a sprinkle of sesame seeds or chili oil for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or pork stock
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 slices of cooked pork or tofu
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved
  • 2 sheets nori
  • Optional: sesame seeds, chili oil

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine chicken or pork stock, soy sauce, miso paste, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Simmer for 30 minutes to develop flavor.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a serving bowl, place the cooked noodles and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with sliced pork or tofu, soft-boiled egg halves, sliced mushrooms, chopped green onions, and a sheet of nori.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds or a drizzle of chili oil if desired, and serve hot.
